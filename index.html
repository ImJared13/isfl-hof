Welcome to the ISFL Hall of Fame!

The ISFL Hall of Fame includes players who have "made outstanding contributions to professional simulation football".

About the Hall of Fame

The charter class of two was selected in 2025 (S10). As of 2068 (S53), 210 individuals have been elected: 169 as players, 16 as general managers, and 23 as users.

Selection Criteria

Enshrinees are selected by a 12-person selection committee, which meets each year at the time and location of the Ultimus Bowl. Nominees must be retired for at least three seasons and have played or coached for a minimum of five seasons to be eligible.

Evolution of Inductions

Initially, only players were eligible. In 2035 (S20), user contributions were considered, and in 2040 (S25), coaches became eligible. As of 2052 (S37), 13 ISFL teams are represented by an enshrinee.

League Affiliation

The Hall is officially affiliated with the International Simulation Football League (ISFL) and does not include any league other than the ISFL. DSFL players are only included if they achieve greatness in the ISFL.
